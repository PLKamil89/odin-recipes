Kharcho

Kharcho is a hearty beef soup in tomato broth with a wonderful blend of spices and plenty of fresh herbs.

The beef is fall-apart tender and just melts in the mouth, which is why the recipe has been adopted and loved by many Georgian families and Easter Europeans alike.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds of beef stew meat
  • 3 teaspoons of salt
  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 celery ribs, finely chopped
  • 4 cups of beef broth + 4 cups of filtered water
  • 800 grams of diced tomatoes
  • 2 dry bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 2 garlic cloves, pressed
  • 1/2 cup of uncooked white rice
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped, plus more to serve
  • 1/4 cup of fresh dill, chopped, plus more to serve

Steps

  1. Sprinkle meat with 3 teaspoons of salt and stir.
  2. Heat a soup pot over medium/high heat and add 2 tablespoons of oil. Once oil is hot, add beef in 2 batches, sautéing until browned on all sides. Remove from pot and set aside.
  3. Add chopped onion and celery with more oil as needed. Saute 5 minutes until softened.
  4. Add canned tomatoes with their juice, 4 cups of broth, 4 cups of water, 2 bay leaves and browned beef. Bring to a boil then reduce heat, cover and simmer 1 and 1/2 to 2 hours or until beef is tender.
  5. Add 1/2 cup of white rice, 2 tablespoons of lemon juice, 2 pressed garlic cloves, 1 teaspoon of coriander, 1 teaspoon of paprika. Cover and simmer 20 minutes or until rice is tender.
  6. Remove from heat and add 1/4 cup of parsley and 1/4 cup of dill then cover and let sit off heat another 10 minutes before serving.
  7. Season to taste with salt and pepper if needed and serve with more fresh herbs as desired.
