Chakapuli


Chakapuli is a very popular lamb stew which traditionally prepared for Easter. It requires sour plums or tomatillos, which give the dish its tangy flavor.

lamb can be substituted with veal or pork but wine, tarragon, and scallions are a must.


Ingredients

  • 2 pounds lamb (with bones), cut into pieces
  • 5 medium size tomatillos, chopped
  • 2 limes
  • 2 bunches of tarragon, stems removed, roughly chopped
  • 2 cups of dry white wine
  • Salt, pepper,and chili flakes

Steps

  1. Place the meat in a medium, wide pot in one layer.
  2. Add a third of the herbs, tomatillos, and red pepper flakes.
  3. Add another layer of meat and then herbs.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper, drizzle the wine and lime juice.
  5. Cover and let it cook over medium-low heat for 45 to 50 minutes until the meat is tender.
  6. Add the rest of the herbs, taste and adjust the seasonings.
